How to subscribe on iPhone

  1. Open Settings → Calendar → Accounts
  2. Tap Add Account → Other
  3. Tap Add Subscribed Calendar
  4. Paste the URL above and tap Next
  5. Tap Save

Game times display in your local timezone automatically. Live scores update within ~15 minutes of a score change. The 🏈 banner at the top of each day shows when the calendar was last refreshed.
